Are we a match?
We would love the opportunity to work with you, but we understand that you may have some additional questions. This quick Q&A covers a lot of the basics. If you have any additional questions, don't hesitate to reach out.
- How long does it take to build my website?
Similar to questions regarding website cost, the timeline will vary greatly based on the scope of your web design project. Typically, our end-to-end build projects take 1-4 months to complete.
+ Can you work with my existing Marketing team?
Absolutely. It’s common for our web development company to collaborate with internal or external digital marketing teams. Throughout the course of a project, we often help these teams set a better structural foundation for SEO, set up smarter segmentation, and improve goal- and event-tracking. Post-launch, we can collaborate with your marketing team to produce new landing pages, website sections, and lead magnets, or we can assist with aspects of the marketing work ourselves.
+ What is the typical relationship between a client and an agency?
The core of our team consists of 1 project manager, 1 creative director, 3 web designers, 4 web developers, 1 copywriter, 1 digital marketer and 1 quality assurance specialist. Apart from this, we can add additional web designers or web developers depending on the size of your web design project. We can also add additional staff to specialize in illustrations or animated graphics, depending on your needs. Customers can look forward to daily e-mails with their team and once-a-week “all in the workplace” meetings via video conferencing. Ultimately, we try to make ourselves as accessible as possible, but at the same time leave room for meaningful progress in our clients' projects.
+ Where is your team located?
Our web design & development team is based in Ukraine and USA. Our headquarters is located in Kyiv, and we have company representation in Cheyenne, WY. We also have remote employees in all over the world.
+ What size and type of companies do you work with?
We have an even balance of start-ups, small businesses, non-profits, and enterprise clients across a multitude of industries. To get a better sense of our wide range of web design projects, check out our client's list.
+ Will you be there for me after the website is complete?
Absolutely. After launch, we interact with customers in two ways: The support team keeps your site up to date, as well as keeping it safe and fast. Depending on the development plan you choose, we will continue to collaborate on developing additional pages and adding new features, SEO, SEM, and so on. Also, as part of the Prime plan, you have access to 24/7 online support.

+ Why do you offer packaged solutions?
The main problem of web development is that the customer does not understand how much the full development and promotion of the site will cost. Therefore, we offer the easiest option for the client, which ensures transparency in the relationship. In addition, packaged solutions for business provide comprehensiveness in the development and promotion of goods and services, which eliminates the need to contact many companies.
